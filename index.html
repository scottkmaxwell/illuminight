Experience Illuminight.

Let's put a little magic into night lights.

Our mission is to scare away the monsters young children may fear
by immersing them in a story that doubles as a night light.

However, Illuminight can be used as a white noise machine to aid troubled sleepers of an older age as well.

Gently introduce to children of young ages that may be frightened by Illuminight at first exposure.

Illuminight is designed for anyone age three and above, but can be used with caution with those younger.

DO NOT: eat, sit on, use in water, drop, shake, put loose hair near motor, or stare directly at the light.
