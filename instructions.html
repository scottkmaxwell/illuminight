TELL A STORY

EFFORTLESSLY.

IT IS TRULY SIMPLE TO USE.


1. CHOOSE A STORY DISC

2. PLACE ON MOUNT

3. FLIP THE SWITCH AND PUSH THE LIGHT

BUT IF YOU NEED MORE DETAILS...

Step 1

Select a story disk.

Step 2

Take off lid.

Step 3

Place disc on gear platform face down, allowing the magnets to lock by lining up the blue lines on the gear platform and disc.

Step 4

Flip the switch that is located on the side that says "K'NEX".

Step 5

Push the light down on the area covered in black tape.

Step 6

Replace lid.

Step 7

Gently place on stable surface in an area with an open ceiling.

Step 8

Lie down in bed and watch the pretty images scare away the darkness.
